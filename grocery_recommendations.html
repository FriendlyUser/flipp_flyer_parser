Things to Consider When Choosing:

  • Over Limit Items: Pay attention to "OVER LIMIT PAY" prices. They only make sense if you need a small quantity.
  • PC Optimum Points: The PC Optimum points deals can add extra value if you're a member and regularly shop at Loblaws/Superstore. Factor in the points value when comparing to other prices.
  • Your Needs: Prioritize items you will actually use and enjoy to avoid waste.
  • Perishable Items: Only buy quantities of fresh produce, meat, and dairy that you can consume before they expire.
